Allocate financial resources

Every year, each PN student is provided with VND 100,000,000/year. The children are fully supported from their study expenses to their meals. Monthly, PN students in Vietnam are provided with 1 million VND/month. The money helps poor students in Vietnam buy daily food and personal items To prepare students to become independent and self-reliant young adults, we have built a personal development program around the PN values: Trust, Responsibility, Solidarity, Respect and Zero Stop Innovating. When studying at PN, students will live together in dormitories, near the training center. They will be self-employed and responsible for their own lives, from managing finances, including spending on food, electricity, water, division of labor to resolving internal conflicts or providing support. help each other. With the goal of helping students fully focus on their studies, PN covers all expenses including: Training costs: tuition fees, remedial classes, equipment, etc. Cost of living: housing and equipment costs, meals, transportation (each student receives a bicycle and a helmet), health care and medical insurance.
